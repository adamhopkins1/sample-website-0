Founders

Learn enough to be a Film Buff was founded in 2020 by Adam Hopkins and Elena Peddicord. We believe that by personalizing and getting others not interested in films to become inspired and excited about the past, present and future of the filmaking medium with our courses which are designed to be exciting fullfilling and above all immersive to let learners engage and feel compelled to want to research and appreciate why filmaking is the most culturally powerful forms of expression today.
