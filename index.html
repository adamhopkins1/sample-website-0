Background

The program consist of an intense course structure of USC affiliated film courses that was created in collaboration witth the USC Film School faculty and instructors for accreditation and also to provide an world class insight and Filmaking from applicable real world examples. We are also offering college-level courses that will be accesible and at the same time offering classic and modern real world application of filmkaking. We believe providing the most important learning should come from those experts in the field and with our courses we are offering multidisciplinary crossdisciplinary structures such as film, cinema, and sociological, humanities, theological,philsophical backgrounds to help bridge multiple gaps for learners to fully gain an understanding of film and its impact on the general worldview. Learners will be able to understand how film is the voice of the common and the uncommon people and how important voice is when either directing , screenwriting, or producing film projects.Ultimately the goal described will help to learners holistically grow in their understanding and relationship with film and filmaking that can be applied even outside the studio lots and locations of films.

Founders

Learn enough to be a Film Buff was founded in 2020 by Adam Hopkins and Elena Peddicord. We believe that by personalizing and getting others not interested in films to become inspired and excited about the past, present and future of the filmaking medium with our courses which are designed to be exciting fullfilling and above all immersive to let learners engage and feel compelled to want to research and appreciate why filmaking is the most culturally powerful forms of expression today.
